HCUS Series

Aeirfix Hepa- Carbon Ultraclean System is a uniquely designed portable system for healthcare application by creating ultra-clean airflow in critical zones like ICU, Isolation Rooms, General wards, Operating rooms etc. The Four stage system destroys bacteria, viruses, fungi, molds, and other pollutants and is ideal for maintaining sterility in the rooms. The system is equipped for 5 stage speed control for regulating required airflow. After Filtration and UV Germicidal removal, the air stream passes through Activated carbon filter designed for odor control and finally through HEPA filter with 99.99% collection efficiency.

Why HCUS?

UV Light Protection
Ultra violet lamp stage destroys airborne contaminants and germs effectively. UV lamps are integrated in the system without direct exposure to the user.

Activated Carbon Filter
Activated Carbon Filter remove contaminants and impurities, utilizing chemical adsorption. This stage captures cigarette smoke, fumes, gases and odor.

Washable Pre Filter
Anti- Bacterial and Anti Mildew Pre-Filter clears large particles. It removes dust, pollen, mildew, and animalcules. This stage also traps particles and animal dander thereby enhancing purity and efficiency.

HEPA Filter
Our Lab tested HEPA filters are among the most efficient air filter available. These are 99.97% efficient and can remove as small as 0.3 microns (Tested as per IEST-RP_CC001.3). Air passage through this stage removes dust, pollens, various bacteria, mold spores and airborne particles.
